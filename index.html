2024 Formula One Teams. Top 4

In the exciting world of Formula 1, the 2024 season has been marked by the battle between titans. Even though the season has just begun, four teams are positioned as the strongest on the grid. F1 in 2024 promises to be exciting and unpredictable. Each race presents new challenges and opportunities for the teams. The fight for glory is on, and only time will tell who will be crowned the best team of the year.

F1 TEAMS 2024

Located in Milton Keynes, United Kingdom. Red Bull has Max Verstappen (Netherlands) and Sergio Pérez (Mexican) as drivers. The team has won 6 Constructors' championships and 7 Drivers' titles.
Its main headquarters is in Maranello, Italy. Ferrari has Charles Leclerc (Monégasque) and Carlos Sainz Jr (Spanish) as main drivers. As a team they have won 16 Constructors' titles and 15 Drivers' titles.
McLaren is headquartered in Woking, United Kingdom. Its drivers are Lando Norris (British) and Oscar Piastri (Australian). In their history they have stood out for having 20 Constructors' titles and 12 Pilots' titles.
Located at Headquarters Brackley, United Kingdom. Mercedes has its main drivers who are Lewis Hamilton (British) and George Russell (British). As a team they have 8 Constructors' titles and 8 Drivers' titles.
